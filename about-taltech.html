    About TalTech

    Tallinn University of Technology (TalTech) is the only technical university in Estonia. It was established in 1918 and is one of the most prestigious universities in the country. TalTech is the flagship of Estonian engineering and technology education.

    The university offers a wide range of study programs in engineering, information technology, economics, business, and maritime studies. With over 14,000 students, including more than 600 international students from 60 countries, TalTech provides a diverse and multicultural learning environment.

    Study Programs

    TalTech offers bachelor's, master's, and doctoral programs across various fields:

    • Engineering: Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Chemical Engineering
    • Information Technology: Computer Science, Cyber Security, Software Engineering, Data Science
    • Economics and Business: Business Administration, Economics, Finance, Innovation Management
    • Maritime Studies: Marine Engineering, Navigation, Port Management
    • Other Fields: Architecture, Design, Applied Sciences
